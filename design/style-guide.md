# SmartNest Brand Style Guide

## Brand Essence
**Tagline**: "Where Comfort Meets Intelligence"

**Mission**: Making smart home technology feel as warm and inviting as the homes they enhance.

**Values**:
- Warmth over coldness
- Simplicity over complexity  
- Trust over hype
- Human over robotic

## Visual Language

### Color Philosophy
Our palette draws from natural materials and warm lighting to create a cozy, high-tech aesthetic:

**Primary Colors**:
- **Copper (#B87333)**: Our signature color - sophisticated, warm metal
- **Amber (#FFB347)**: Represents warm lighting and active states

**Supporting Colors**:
- **Sage (#7C9070)**: Natural, calming, success states
- **Terracotta (#D4735F)**: Earthy warmth, error states
- **Warm Black (#0F0E0C)**: Rich, cozy backgrounds
- **Cream (#FAF8F5)**: Soft, readable text

**Usage**:
- Copper for primary actions and branding
- Amber for active states and highlights
- Sage for success and eco-features
- Dark backgrounds to reduce eye strain

### Typography

**Display (Manrope)**:
- Headlines and marketing copy
- Modern but approachable
- Weights: 400, 600, 700

**Body (DM Sans)**:
- UI text and descriptions
- Exceptional readability
- Weights: 400, 500

**Data (JetBrains Mono)**:
- Prices, measurements, codes
- Clear number distinction
- Weight: 400

### Visual Effects

**Glow Effects**:
- Copper glow on primary actions
- Amber glow on active devices
- Subtle, not overwhelming

**Glass Morphism**:
- Elevated panels and modals
- Creates depth without heaviness
- Always with warm tints

**LED Indicators**:
- Mimic real device LEDs
- Pulse animation for life
- Status communication

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
**Warm Professional**: Like a knowledgeable friend who happens to be a tech expert.

**Do**:
- "Let's get your home connected"
- "Your living room is now 20% more energy efficient"
- "Everything's working perfectly"

**Don't**:
- "Initiate setup protocol"
- "Error code 4033"
- "Syncing nodes to mesh network"

### Content Principles

1. **Human First**: Explain benefits before features
   - ✓ "Wake up to the perfect temperature"
   - ✗ "Programmable 7-day scheduling system"

2. **Plain Language**: Avoid jargon
   - ✓ "Works with your other devices"
   - ✗ "Zigbee 3.0 mesh protocol compatible"

3. **Encouraging**: Make users feel capable
   - ✓ "You're all set! Here's what's next..."
   - ✗ "Configuration complete. Proceed to next step."

4. **Warm Welcome**: Every interaction should feel inviting
   - ✓ "Welcome home! Your devices are ready."
   - ✗ "System online. Status: operational."

## Photography & Imagery

### Style Direction
- Warm, lived-in homes (not sterile showrooms)
- Natural lighting, golden hour preferred
- People enjoying comfort, not staring at devices
- Devices blend into home, not dominate

### Product Shots
- Show devices in context
- Highlight the warm glow of displays
- Include lifestyle elements (plants, books, coffee)
- Avoid stark white backgrounds

## Motion & Animation

### Principles
- **Purposeful**: Every animation has meaning
- **Smooth**: Nothing jarring or robotic
- **Subtle**: Enhance, don't distract

### Standard Animations
- **Fade in**: Content appears gently (300ms)
- **Scale up**: Buttons respond to interaction (200ms)
- **Glow pulse**: LEDs and active states (2s loop)
- **Smooth scroll**: Navigation feels fluid

## Layout Principles

### Spacing
- Generous whitespace for breathing room
- Consistent rhythm using 8px grid
- Tighter on mobile, spacious on desktop

### Hierarchy
- Clear distinction between levels
- Important actions stand out with copper
- Secondary info in muted colors

### Cards & Containers
- Rounded corners (8-16px) for softness
- Subtle borders, not harsh lines
- Glass effects for elevation

## Icon Guidelines

### Style
- Rounded, friendly shapes
- 2px stroke weight
- Consistent 24px grid
- Warm accent colors

### Usage
- Support text, don't replace it
- Active states show amber
- Animate on interaction

## Application Examples

### Marketing
- Lead with comfort and lifestyle
- Show technology as invisible helper
- Focus on family and relaxation

### Product Pages
- Interactive elements to "try"
- Clear, benefit-focused features
- Trust signals and guarantees

### Support
- Friendly troubleshooting
- Visual guides over text walls
- "We're here to help" tone

## Don'ts

- Cold, clinical aesthetics
- Technical jargon in main content
- Stark white backgrounds
- Sharp, aggressive angles
- Robotic or impersonal language
- Overwhelming animations
- Generic stock photography
- Complex navigation patterns

## Implementation Notes

This design system prioritizes:
1. **Warmth**: Every element should feel inviting
2. **Clarity**: Users never feel confused
3. **Trust**: Professional but approachable
4. **Delight**: Small moments of joy

Remember: We're not just selling smart home devices, we're helping people create warmer, more comfortable homes that happen to be intelligent.
